Event Description

ATEEZ - ATEEZ is a South Korean boy band formed by KQ Entertainment, recognized for their high-energy performances and diverse musical style.

Debuting in 2018, the group quickly gained international attention with their powerful choreography, charismatic stage presence, and a unique blend of genres including K-pop, hip-hop, EDM, and rock.

Their music often features themes of adventure, youth, and personal growth, resonating with a wide audience.

Known for their elaborate music videos and concept-driven albums, ATEEZ has built a strong global fanbase, making significant waves in the K-pop industry and beyond.
