Tomorrow X Together

TXT - TXT, short for Tomorrow X Together, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment, known for their vibrant music and relatable storytelling.

Debuting in 2019, the group quickly garnered attention with their fresh sound that blends pop, rock, and R&B elements.

TXT's music often explores themes of youth, dreams, and the challenges of growing up, resonating deeply with a younger audience.

Their intricate choreography, visually stunning music videos, and strong vocal performances have earned them a substantial international fanbase.

With a series of successful albums and a distinct artistic identity, TXT continues to establish themselves as a prominent force in the K-pop industry. Don't miss the electrifying TXTi.
