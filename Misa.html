ENHYPEN - ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy band formed by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE Corporation.

Debuting in 2020, the group emerged from the reality survival show "I-LAND," which showcased their talent and determination.

Known for their captivating performances, ENHYPEN blends various musical styles, including pop, rock, and hip-hop, creating a dynamic and versatile sound.

Their music often delves into themes of self-discovery, growth, and connection, resonating with a wide audience.

With their striking visuals, synchronized choreography, and strong vocal abilities, ENHYPEN has quickly gained a substantial global fanbase, establishing themselves as a rising force in the K-pop scene.
