Music Events

Wave to Earth -Wave to Earth is a South Korean indie band known for their soothing blend of jazz, indie pop, and alternative music. The band's sound is characterized by its mellow instrumentation, thoughtful lyrics, and dreamy vocals, creating a tranquil and emotive listening experience. With their minimalist yet intricate arrangements, Wave to Earth crafts music that feels both intimate and expansive, resonating deeply with listeners. Their artistic approach and unique sound have garnered a dedicated following, establishing them as a distinctive presence in the indie music scene. A mesmerizing concert with indie vibes.
THE POLES -The Poles is an emerging indie rock band known for their vibrant energy and catchy melodies. Their music blends elements of rock, punk, and pop, resulting in an infectious and dynamic sound that appeals to a wide audience. Characterized by driving guitar riffs, engaging lyrics, and spirited performances, The Poles have quickly built a reputation for their lively concerts and genuine connection with fans. As they continue to release new music and perform, The Poles are carving out a niche for themselves in the indie music landscape, attracting attention for their raw talent and authentic style. Experience the magic of THE POLES live.
Stray Kids - Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment, known for their powerful performances and self-produced music. Debuting in 2018, the group quickly gained recognition for their intense energy, intricate choreography, and unique sound, which blends elements of hip-hop, electronic, and pop. Comprised of talented members who contribute to songwriting and production, Stray Kids often explore themes of personal growth, resilience, and youthful struggles in their music. Their dynamic stage presence and charismatic appeal have earned them a dedicated global fanbase, establishing them as one of the leading acts in the K-pop industry.Get ready for an electrifying performance by Stray Kids.
ATEEZ - ATEEZ is a South Korean boy band formed by KQ Entertainment, recognized for their high-energy performances and diverse musical style. Debuting in 2018, the group quickly gained international attention with their powerful choreography, charismatic stage presence, and a unique blend of genres including K-pop, hip-hop, EDM, and rock. Their music often features themes of adventure, youth, and personal growth, resonating with a wide audience. Known for their elaborate music videos and concept-driven albums, ATEEZ has built a strong global fanbase, making significant waves in the K-pop industry and beyond.Join ATEEZ for an unforgettable night of music.
PARTYNEXTDOOR - PARTYNEXTDOOR, born Jahron Anthony Brathwaite, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer who has significantly influenced the contemporary R&B and hip-hop scenes. Known for his smooth, atmospheric sound and introspective lyrics, PARTYNEXTDOOR blends elements of R&B, hip-hop, and electronic music to create a distinctively moody and evocative style. As one of the first artists signed to Drake's OVO Sound label, he has written and produced for numerous high-profile artists while also releasing his own critically acclaimed projects, such as "PARTYNEXTDOOR" and "PARTYNEXTDOOR 2." His work is characterized by its emotive melodies and innovative production, making him a standout figure in modern music.Don't miss PARTYNEXTDOOR's thrilling live show.
Travis Scott - Travis Scott, born Jacques Webster II, is a renowned American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer known for his genre-blending music and dynamic stage presence. Hailing from Houston, Texas, Scott's innovative sound combines elements of hip-hop, psychedelic rock, and trap, marked by his distinctive auto-tuned vocals and immersive production. His critically acclaimed albums, such as "Astroworld," have cemented his status in the music industry, while his high-energy live performances and spectacular stage designs have set new standards for concerts. Beyond music, Scott's ventures into fashion and business, including his collaborations with major brands, showcase his multifaceted influence on contemporary culture.Experience the energy of Travis Scott live in concert.
Yeat - Yeat, an American rapper and songwriter born Noah Olivier Smith, has rapidly gained recognition for his distinctive blend of trap and experimental hip-hop. Known for his unique vocal style and energetic performances, Yeat incorporates a mix of melodic hooks and hard-hitting beats that captivate his audience. Emerging from the underground scene, his music often features themes of hedonism and personal struggle, resonating with a diverse and growing fanbase. With a rapidly expanding discography and collaborations with notable artists, Yeat continues to carve out a unique space in the contemporary rap landscape.Catch Yeat's unique sound in a live performance.
TXT - TXT, short for Tomorrow X Together, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment, known for their vibrant music and relatable storytelling. Debuting in 2019, the group quickly garnered attention with their fresh sound that blends pop, rock, and R&B elements. TXT's music often explores themes of youth, dreams, and the challenges of growing up, resonating deeply with a younger audience. Their intricate choreography, visually stunning music videos, and strong vocal performances have earned them a substantial international fanbase. With a series of successful albums and a distinct artistic identity, TXT continues to establish themselves as a prominent force in the K-pop industry.Don't miss the electrifying TXTi.
ENHYPEN - ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy band formed by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE Corporation. Debuting in 2020, the group emerged from the reality survival show "I-LAND," which showcased their talent and determination. Known for their captivating performances, ENHYPEN blends various musical styles, including pop, rock, and hip-hop, creating a dynamic and versatile sound. Their music often delves into themes of self-discovery, growth, and connection, resonating with a wide audience. With their striking visuals, synchronized choreography, and strong vocal abilities, ENHYPEN has quickly gained a substantial global fanbase, establishing themselves as a rising force in the K-pop scene.Join ENHYPEN for an unforgettable performance.
DPR IAN - DPR Ian, also known as Christian Yu, is a multifaceted artist hailing from South Korea. As the founder of the creative collective DPR (Dream Perfect Regime), Ian has made significant strides in the realms of music production, directing, and visual arts. His solo music often explores a fusion of genres, including hip-hop, R&B, and electronic music, characterized by smooth beats, introspective lyrics, and innovative production. With his distinct artistic vision and meticulous attention to detail, Ian has garnered a dedicated following both in South Korea and internationally. Beyond his music, Ian's talents extend to directing music videos and visual content, showcasing his versatility and creativity across various mediums.Experience DPR IAN's dynamic live show.
